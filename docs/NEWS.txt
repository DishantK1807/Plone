Changelog
=========

3.3a1 - Unreleased
------------------

- Remove CatalogTool.ExtensibleIndexableObjectWrapper in favour of the wrapper
  in plone.indexer, and make registerIndexableAttribute() a deprecated facade
  for registering an IIndexer adapter. See plone.indexer for more information
  about the correct (and more robust) way to register custom indexers.
  [optilude]
